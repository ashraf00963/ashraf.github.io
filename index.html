Q: How do I clean and maintain my OvenEco oven to ensure optimal performance?

A: To keep your OvenEco oven in top condition, regularly wipe down the interior with a damp cloth and mild detergent. Remove any spills promptly to prevent residue buildup. Refer to the user manual for specific cleaning instructions. For maintenance, ensure the oven is unplugged before performing any tasks.
